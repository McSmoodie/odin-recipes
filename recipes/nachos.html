How to Make Delicious Nachos!

Ingredients:

  • Tortilla chips
  • Chicken mince
  • Spice mix
  • Guacamole
  • Sour cream
  • Cheese

Steps:

  1. Cook chicken mince with the spice mix.
  2. Lay out the chips on a plate and cover with cheese.
  3. Put the cooked mince on top of the chips so that the cheese melts.
  4. Top with sour cream and then guacamole.
  5. Enjoy!
